# 🎵 Spotify Global Music Dashboard

A Power BI project analysing global Spotify track data to uncover music trends, 
artist popularity, and genre patterns over time.

---



## 🔍 Project Overview

This was my first end-to-end Power BI project that built from raw Spotify track data 
through to a fully interactive dashboard. The goal was to explore what makes a track 
or artist successful globally, and how music trends have shifted over two decades.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used

- **Power BI** — Dashboard design and interactive visuals
- **Power Query** — Data cleaning and preparation
- **DAX** — Calculated measures for KPIs and Top-N analysis
- **Data Modelling** — Structuring relationships across the dataset

---

## 📌 Key Features

- **KPI Cards** — Total Tracks (8,776), Average Popularity (52.24), Total Artists (2,548)
- **Monthly Popularity Heatmap** — Popularity scores across months and years (2000–present)
- **Top 10 Artists by Popularity** — Ranked bar chart with dynamic filtering
- **Top 5 Genres by Average Popularity** — Line trend across years
- **Trend Analysis** — Music releases and popularity over time
- **Explicit Content Breakdown** — Track volume split by explicit vs clean content

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Pop, rap, and soft pop consistently rank among the highest-performing genres
- Music release volume grew significantly from 2015 onwards
- Popularity scores show seasonal variation, with certain months performing stronger
- Top artists by popularity are not always the most prolific in track count
